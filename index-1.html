About Verdant

Verdant works with global manufacturing and industrial technology companies to help them enter, scale, and succeed in India — and with Indian OEMs and manufacturers to access differentiated global technologies and partners.

India is one of the world's most opportunity-rich and execution-intensive manufacturing markets. Success here requires more than market reports or channel partners.

It requires operating experience, credibility, and a deep understanding of how decisions are made on the ground. Verdant exists to provide exactly that.

The Principal

Operator-Led.
Relationship-Driven.

Verdant is led by Nidhi Shah, an operator who has introduced, built, and scaled entirely new manufacturing technologies in India, with over two decades of experience across Indian and international markets.

Across her career, Nidhi has been responsible for bringing advanced manufacturing technologies to India at a time when they were largely unknown — shaping market understanding, building credibility, and guiding customers through adoption until these technologies became established and commercially viable.

She has worked extensively across India, Europe, the US, ASEAN, and ANZ, and has held senior leadership roles at ExOne Global Holdings (erstwhile voxeljet AG), Imaginarium Solutions, and in multiple market-entry and advisory engagements.
